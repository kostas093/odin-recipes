Grilled Greek Potatoes

Description

These grilled potatoes are great with souvlaki. All you need is a Greek salad for a full meal! They can be prepared hours before grilling.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning

Steps

  1. Pierce potatoes all over with a fork. Place potatoes in the microwave and cook until soft, about 15 minutes.
  2. Mix lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and Greek seasoning together in a small bowl.
  3. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise and score the flesh. Brush on the seasoned oil.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Place potatoes flesh-side down and grill until browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
